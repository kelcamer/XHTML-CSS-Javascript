What is Business?
People who like to work indoors, like to work with people and ideas and have good leadership skills are often attracted to jobs in business. Business graduates can be found in almost every industry doing things like managing operations, hiring and training workers, ordering supplies and merchandise, and managing computer systems.
Business Job Market
  • For workers in management, business, and financial occupations, employment is expected to increase 13.6 percent by 2010
  • Among business and financial occupations, accountants and auditors, and management analysts will add the most new jobs, 326,000 combined.
  • Office and administrative support occupations are projected to create 7.7 million job openings by 2010, ranking third behind service and professional related occupations.
