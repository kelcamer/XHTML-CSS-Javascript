All about Me and My Family

   My name is Kelsey Cameron. I was born at Winter Springs Hospital on February 22, 1996 at 5:29 A.M. and 26 seconds. As I grew older I attended Tuskawilla Montessori in preschool, Arbor Ridge for Kindergarten and 1st grade, and then I moved to Oviedo, and went to Rainbow Elementary for the remainder of Elementary School, and soon after I transitioned to Indian Trails Middle School; and experienced Middle school for the first time, and yet again finally transferred to Oviedo High School as a freshman this year.
