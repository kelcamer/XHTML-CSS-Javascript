Splash

Arcadium's water rides constitute a park within the park. You can experience the thrill of a 8-story water slide. Or enjoy a twisting, curving ride through one of our water tubes. For a ride gentler on the stomach, take a few laps on a lazy river in a one-, two-, or four-person inner tube. No day is complete without a visit of one of our two wavepools.

Be sure to visit the following water rides ...

The Big Dipper
Test your nerves with this classic water slide - from 8 stories above Arcadium.
The Wavepool
Try one of our two wave pools, with big waves every fifteen minutes.
The Blackhole
A four-slide complex complete with special effects and waterfalls. You'll be sure to leave this black hole afraid of the dark.
Flash Flood
Grab 10 of your friends and hope for the best as your huge boat drops out of sight, creating a mammoth wave at the bottom.
The Lazy River
Ready to relax? Grab a tube and tour Arcadium along this carefree waterway.

So grab your suit, your towel, and join us at Arcadium.
