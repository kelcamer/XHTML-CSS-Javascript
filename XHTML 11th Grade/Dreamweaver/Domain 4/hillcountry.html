The Sauer-Beckmann Living History Farm is located at the Lyndon B. Johnson State Park and Historic Site. This farm recreates farm life in the Hill Country, as it would have been at the beginning of the 20th century.  Daily demonstrations include gathering eggs, cooking on a wood-burning stove, tending to livestock and milking cows.
Also in the area, visitors can visit the LBJ Ranch along the banks of the Pedernales River, the one-room schoolhouse that President Johnson attended, and the Johnson family cemetery.
